About Unit 14

We, (me and my classmate) were given the fictional scenario to plan and build a pc specifically designed for the requirements of the company.

How and why i choose the parts

We were tasked with planning and 'making' a pc for the companys specifications withch were that its a office comuter and so i made a pc with quite fans, low power cpu but enough ram and simple gpu. This is enough to run multiple/large documents with office aplications.

Installing hardware

Installing the hardware was easy, i have made pcs before and we had access to a lot of tools that made it easy to install. The problem was installing a windows operating system, we were provided usbs with windows but many were corrupt and so after getting 1 to work we installed all the others with working installs.

Review of the unit

Unit 14 was a nice break from the digital tasks and let me more explore the physicle aspects of IT and PCS, it wasent that hard of a unit and i enjoyed it.
